Hi! I’m Jenny. I’m a a fourth-year Ph.D. Candidate in the Information Science Department at Cornell University, where I’m fortunate to be advised by Malte Jung in the Robots in Groups Lab. Currently, I am a visiting researcher in the Social Technologies research group at Cornell Tech, working with Mor Naaman. Previously, I was a visiting scholar at Brown HCI, working with Jeff Huang. I design and evaluate the impact of AI-mediated communication tools on people’s emotional and social well-being. With a training background in cognitive science and design research, I applied a wide range of in-person and remote research methodologies, from large-scale surveys to participatory design workshops. One of my current works examined how augmented reality glasses affect social interactions through a human-centered design approach.
